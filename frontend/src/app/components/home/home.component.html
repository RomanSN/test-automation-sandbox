Site rules

A non-logged-in user has full access to the Playground section and limited access to the Articles section.

A non-logged-in user can view existing articles, sort and filter them. A logged-in user has extended access:

the ability to filter only their own articles, create a new article, delete, and edit an existing ones.

The JWT standard is used for authentication/authorization.

Registration rules:

A user can register up to 3 accounts.

Username: 5 - 20 characters

Password: 5 - 20 characters

Articles limitation:

Each account is limited to creating 10 articles.

Title: 5 - 30 characters

Content: 20 - 1000 characters

Prohibited Content Policy:

Any content is prohibited if it

  • Contains profanity, obscenity, or vulgar language
  • Includes hate speech, harassment, or threats
  • Promotes racism, sexism, or any form of discrimination or bigotry
  • Is abusive, defamatory, or incites violence

Violations of this policy may result in immediate removal of content and suspension of your account(s).
